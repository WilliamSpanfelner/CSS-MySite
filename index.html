My Hobbies

Sports

I love all winter sports and enjoy motorbikng, woodworking, rowing, cycling and running. Running for philanthropic causes led me to raise funds for the Look charity and finish the London Marathon. Completing the Athens Authentic Marathon was a proud day for me along with having participated in many other races. Running takes some beating for its convenience, but winter sports and rowing are my true passions. I am a qualified BASI Ski Instructor to Level 1.

Motorbiking

The freedom of touring, the sense of adventure, the shear thrill of acceleration and the fascination of engineering brings motorbiking to life for me. Motorbikes have long been hailed as conduits to making new acquaintances and to that, I can bare witness having met countless individuals from Dunnet Head to the Temple of Afaia.

Woodworking

Wood in all its varieties of texture and color and the flexibility it offers in terms of objects that can be constructed from it, make it a very exciting material. The pleasure derived from hewing a spoon from an olive branch is as unmatched as is designing a tool that will facilitate further constructions.

Cooking

Take basic ingredients and create a meal that invigorates the senses and rejuvenates while embracing the underlying principles of nature, is the attraction for me in the kitchen. Influenced by Raymond Blanc, Heston Blumenthal, Rick Stein, Julia Child and many others from a very early age, my passion for cooking really ignited when I began to understand, appreciate and observe the laws and the gifts of nature. Cooking brings people together.
